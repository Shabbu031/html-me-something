Patan, Lalitpur

"Lalitpur Metropolitan City, historically Patan, is the third largest city of Nepal. I was born in historic place,Patan was founded during the 3rd Century BC by the Kirat dynasty and was later expanded in the sixth century.Lalitpur is also known as Manigal. It is best known for its rich cultural heritage, particularly its tradition of arts and crafts. It is called city of festival and feast, fine ancient art, making of metallic and stone carving statue."
